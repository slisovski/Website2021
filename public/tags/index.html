Simeon Lisovski

Researcher at the Alfred Wegener Institute and the Geo.X Young Academy

I am a quantitative ecologist specialized in the fields of avian migration, theoretical ecology and host-pathogen interactions. My research aims to improve our understanding of the natural world and particularly how animals interact with their rapidely changing biotic and abiotic environments.
