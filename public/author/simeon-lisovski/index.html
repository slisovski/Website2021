Simeon Lisovski

Researcher

Alfred Wegener Institute

Geo.X Network

I am a quantitative ecologist specialized in the fields of avian migration, theoretical ecology and host-pathogen interactions. My research aims to improve our understanding of the natural world and particularly how animals interact with their rapidly changing environments.

Interests
  • Global change impacts on animal migration
  • Seasonality in a changing world
  • Remote sensing analyses in ecology
  • Wildlife disease dynamics
